📋 General Dining Hall Rules

  • Crockery, cutlery, and glasses MAY NOT be removed from the Dining Hall.
  • Students MAY NOT take food out of the Dining Hall except: own portion of fruit of the day and two slices of bread (from the four slices allowed) at the supper meal.
  • No meals will be served in residence rooms without permission from a Warden (only for sick/injured students on temporary basis). If too ill to eat in Dining Hall, report to Health Care Centre.
  • NO ONE is permitted to remove food from the Dining Hall on plates or any type of container.
  • Students are expected to treat the dining hall facilities and staff with respect.
  • NOT allowed in the kitchen unless accompanied by a catering staff member (applies to area behind servery as well). Complaints about food must be reported immediately to server duty or caterer on duty.
  • No bare feet in the dining hall (safety reason – glass breakages).
  • Possession and/or consumption of liquor is STRICTLY PROHIBITED, except at formal dinners. Entering under influence of alcohol is strictly prohibited.
  • Courtesy towards dining hall staff and fellow students is expected at all times.
  • Smoking is NOT permitted within the precinct of the dining hall.
  • You may not share your meal with anyone who does not have a meal booked. Without an extra meal booking, you are NOT entitled to any salads, juice or bread.

🍽️ Meal Allowances (per person)

🌅 Breakfast Allowance
  • 2 glasses of juice OR 1 glass of milk OR 1 fruit juice OR 2 fruit only (no juice)
  • 🍞 4 slices of bread
  • 🧻 2 serviettes
☀️ Lunch Allowance
  • 2 glasses of juice OR 1 fruit and 1 juice OR 2 fruit (no juice)
  • 🍞 4 slices of bread
  • 🧻 2 serviettes
🌙 Supper Allowance
  • 2 glasses of juice OR 1 fruit and 1 pudding OR 1 fruit and 1 juice OR 2 fruit (no juice)
  • 🍞 4 slices of bread
  • 🧻 2 serviettes
🥛 PLEASE DON'T DRINK THE MILK – The milk provided is for tea and coffee only.

🍊 Juice, Fruit & Dessert Combinations

Choose ONE of the following options per meal (where dessert is available):

1 Juice + 1 Fruit 2 Juice + No Fruit 2 Fruit + No Juice 1 Juice + 1 Dessert 2 Juice + No Dessert
❗ 2 DESSERTS ARE NOT PERMITTED! — Strictly one dessert maximum per meal.

⚠️ Sharing Meals & Disciplinary Actions

  • If you have no meal booked, you MAY NOT take seconds. You also MAY NOT take someone else's seconds.
  • Taking seconds without a meal booking will lead to disciplinary action.

🍞 Removal of Food from Dining Hall (Supper only)

  • 4 slices of bread + 1 fruit maximum (NO MORE than 4 slices of bread).
  • If caught taking more than 4 slices of bread → disciplinary action.
  • Condiments, cutlery, and crockery may not be removed from the dining hall.
  • Valid meal bookings are essential to enter the dining hall at mealtimes.

🧂 General Dining Hall Etiquette

  • Treat catering staff with courtesy and respect — they work hard to serve you.
  • Clean up after yourself: return used crockery and cutlery to designated areas.
  • Avoid wasting food — only take what you will eat.
  • Keep noise levels reasonable so everyone can enjoy their meal.
  • If you have a complaint or concern, report it immediately to the caterer on duty (not later on social media).
  • Line up orderly and wait for your turn.
💡 Remember: The dining hall is a shared space. Your cooperation ensures a pleasant environment for everyone.
