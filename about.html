Why Campus Food Voice?

Campus Food Voice was built to give every student a simple, anonymous, and effective way to share feedback about dining hall experiences.

We know that many students feel anxious, shy, or unsure about speaking up in person. Some complaints go unreported, small issues become big problems, and hall representatives often hear about problems too late.

This platform bridges the gap between students and their SRC Hall Representatives and Food Service Managers. Every submission is instantly visible to the responsible admin of that hall, allowing them to:

  • ✅ Respond quickly to complaints or concerns
  • ✅ Flag recurring issues and escalate to the correct department
  • ✅ Acknowledge compliments and positive feedback
  • ✅ Keep a transparent record of all hall feedback

Students can now confidently submit feedback without fear or embarrassment. Your voice matters – and it reaches the right people instantly, anonymously if you choose.

By using real‑time status tracking (with a unique ID), you’ll always know when staff have seen your message and what they replied. No more wondering if anyone listened.

We believe that great campus food comes from great communication. This tool is our contribution to a better, more responsive dining experience for everyone.
